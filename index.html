With you, Robotina

Robotina is here to become the soul of every single house

Robotina is here to turn any conventional house into a smart house. In our project we are using different sensors that will let you know what’s going on in your house 24/7.
Every single corner of your house will be smart, you will receive an alert once someone uses your doorbell and you will be able to open the door regardless if you’re in your bedroom or bathroom. We are working to integrate audio and video functionalities with the doorbell like taking a snap of the person outside the house and send it to you, or initiate a conversation without even being near the door.
